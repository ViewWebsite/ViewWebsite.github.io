The High Jump

The High Jump is an event that involves jumping. You are next to a mat and you jump up. You then fall on the mat. You are trying to jump the highest. Each jump is recorded right after the person jumps.

GCS Middle School Championship Top Hi Jump Distances

  1. 5-04 meters
  2. 5-02 meters
  3. 5-0 meters
  4. 4-10 meters
  5. 4-08 meters
