The 4x4 Relay

The 4 by 4 relay is a relay race in which runners sprint, altough usualy not aas fast as the other relays.. You have 4 runners running. Each runner runs 400 meters. After they complete it, they pass of a baton to the next person, who then does their lap. This goes on until the fourth runner comletes the race. In all 100 meters are run.
