The 4x1 Relay

The 4 by 1 relay is a relay race in which runners sprint. You have 4 runners running. Each runner runs 100 meters. After they complete it, they pass of a baton to the next person, who then does their lap. This goes on until the fourth runner comletes the race. In all 400 meters are run,
