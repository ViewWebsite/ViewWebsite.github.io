The 4x2 Relay

The 4 by 2 relay is a relay race in which runners sprint. You have 4 runners running. Each runner runs 200 meters. After they complete it, they pass of a baton to the next person, who then does their lap. This goes on until the fourth runner comletes the race. In all 800 meters are run.
